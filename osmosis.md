# Osmosis - Purpose & Features

## What is Osmosis?

Osmosis is a **personal knowledge management and reflection app** designed to help you capture, organize, and internalize learning from your stories and your own thinking. It transforms passive reading into active learning through highlighting, note-taking, and reflection.

The app emphasizes *continuous personal growth* by creating a permanent record of your intellectual journey.

---

## Core Philosophy

Learning isn't just about consuming information—it's about:
- **Capturing** insights as they arise
- **Reflecting** on what things mean to you personally
- **Connecting** ideas across different sources
- **Evolving** your understanding over time

Osmosis is built around this cycle, giving you tools for each step.

---

## Main Features

### 1. Story Management & Reading
**Store and read your knowledge library**

- Add stories to your local library with a title, summary, and full content
- Read stories with a clean, distraction-free interface
- Mark stories as read/unread
- Favorite stories for quick access
- Search across all your stories

Creating a story is intentionally simple — just **Title**, **Summary**, and **Content** (Markdown supported). There is no categorization or domain to manage.

### 2. Annotation System
**Capture insights while reading**

The annotation system includes three ways to capture from your stories:

#### Highlights
- Highlight key passages from the story text
- Auto-saves with the text you selected
- Appears in the timeline with visual formatting

#### Notes
- Add your own thoughts alongside highlighted text
- Combine a personal note with a supporting quote
- Tap a notated word in the story to see the note you wrote

#### Bookmarks
- Mark important sections for later reference
- Quick way to flag content without detailed notes

All annotations:
- Save instantly to your timeline
- Include date and story reference
- Can be favorited, edited, or deleted (bookmarks can be deleted)
- Searchable in the timeline and the notes workstation

### 3. Deep Reflection Interface
**Process and synthesize what you learn**

Accessed from the **Dashboard** as the *Reflection Tracker* card, the reflection tool provides:
- **Socratic prompts** - Get inspiring questions to spark deeper thinking
- **Freeform writing** - Draft your raw thoughts and insights
- **Undo/Redo** - Full editing history for your reflections
- **Reflection persistence** - All reflections saved to the timeline automatically

Use this to:
- Synthesize concepts in your own words
- Connect ideas across multiple stories
- Develop your own perspective
- Record insights for future reference

### 4. Dashboard
**Your learning journey at a glance**

The Dashboard is the home for your progress and tools, organized as expandable cards:

- **Overview Stats** - Stories, highlights, notes, and reflections at a glance
- **Activity Heatmap** - A 28-day view of your engagement
- **Reflection Tracker** - Write and review your reflections
- **Serendipity Engine** - Rediscover random insights

### 5. Timeline
**Your complete intellectual journey**

A chronological record of everything:

#### What Gets Recorded
- Highlights from stories
- Personal notes
- Bookmarks
- Reflections you write
- Stories you read

#### Timeline Features
- **Filtering** - By type (Favorites, Notes, Highlights, Bookmarks, Reflections, Read Stories)
- **Search** - Find anything in your learning history
- **Favorites** - Tap the heart to favorite an item; it colors instantly and you can filter to show only favorites
- **Zoom levels** - View by day, week, or milestones
- **Editing** - Edit or delete items (notes/highlights/reflections are editable; bookmarks can be deleted)
- **Multi-select** - Bulk operations on multiple items

#### Why It Matters
The timeline creates a **permanent record** of your growth. You can:
- Look back and see how your thinking evolved
- Rediscover forgotten insights
- Track patterns in what you learn

### 6. Serendipity Engine
**Rediscover random insights**

Accessed from the **Dashboard**, it randomly surfaces a story or note from your library to:
- Spark new connections between ideas
- Remind you of past learnings
- Break out of linear thinking

### 7. Search & Discovery
**Find what you need, when you need it**

Multiple search interfaces:
- **Global search** - Search across all stories by title and content
- **Timeline search** - Find any item in your learning history
- **Notes search** - Find highlights and annotations

Input fields include Copy/Paste buttons for quick text entry and real-time filtering.

### 8. Fast Content Entry
**Paste content without typing everything**

Copy/Paste buttons on every create field:
- **Title field**
- **Summary field**
- **Content field**

Just paste content and refine it rather than retyping.

### 9. Themes & Customization

#### Dark/Light Mode
- Toggle between dark and light themes
- Customizable accent and reading colors
- Adjustable story reading size and background color

#### Mobile-First Design
- Optimized for mobile devices
- Touch gestures (swipe to close)
- Long-press context menus
- Responsive layout that adapts to any screen

---

## Navigation

The bottom bar gives you direct access to the main areas:
- **Explore** - Browse and read your stories
- **Timeline** - Your chronological record
- **Dashboard** - Stats, heatmap, reflection, serendipity
- **Create** - Add a new story
- **Settings** (gear icon) - Themes, reading preferences, data import/export

---

## Data Storage

**All data is stored locally in your browser** using localStorage:
- No cloud sync
- No accounts required
- Your data stays private
- Can export/import your library

---

## Typical Usage Flow

### Reading & Learning
1. Add a story to your library (title, summary, content)
2. Read and highlight key passages
3. Add your own notes next to important quotes
4. Bookmark sections for later

### Processing & Reflection
1. Open the Reflection Tracker from the Dashboard
2. Answer Socratic questions to deepen understanding
3. Connect ideas across multiple stories

### Discovering & Connecting
1. Browse your timeline to see your learning journey
2. Use the Serendipity Engine to rediscover forgotten insights
3. Find patterns and connections across your learning
