Welcome to Osmosis

❦
“Read. Mark. Reflect. Remember.”

Osmosis turns reading into lasting knowledge. You capture what matters, think it through, and revisit it over time — building a private library that compounds. This quick tour shows you everything it can do.

Your Shelf

The Library

The Raven
6 min · 3 notes

Each story card shows its read time, a progress bar, and your notes. Finished ones dim so the unread stand out. Tap Create to write your own — a title, a hook, and content (Markdown works).

While Reading

Select text to mark it

Deep into that darkness peering, long I stood there wondering…
✎Highlight ✎Note ⚑Mark ADefine

Select any passage to Highlight it, attach a Note, drop a Bookmark, or Define a word. A highlight carrying a note gets a dotted underline — tap it to reread your thought.
